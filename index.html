Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Trading & Contracting

Projects Path General Trading & Contracting is a Baghdad-based company providing end-to-end solutions across construction, energy, and trade. We combine local market knowledge with international standards to deliver projects that stand the test of time.

From civil construction and oil & gas support services to the import and export of construction materials and the deployment of photovoltaic solar systems, our multidisciplinary teams manage every stage with safety, quality, and accountability at the core.

  • Licensed and registered general contractor
  • Qualified engineers, technicians & project managers
  • Commitment to HSE and international quality standards
  • Reliable supply chain and trusted partners

Our Mission

To deliver dependable, high-quality projects and supplies that drive Iraq's infrastructure and energy development forward.

Our Vision

To be a leading integrated trading and contracting group in Iraq, recognized for engineering excellence and trusted partnerships.

Our Values

Integrity, safety, quality, and a relentless focus on client success in every engagement.
